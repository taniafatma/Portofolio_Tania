My Project

Online Library Website

This online library website is specifically designed for elementary school students. In this Online Library, students can explore a variety of reading materials and gain access to renowned learning platforms across various genres, themes, and subjects. For students looking to improve their literacy skills, this website provides engaging and interactive reading materials. These resources are carefully selected to enhance comprehension, vocabulary, and critical thinking abilities. Additionally, this website has a dedicated section that showcases books and materials focused on mathematics and English language subjects. Students can access math-related books that improve problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and numerical understanding. Similarly, our English language collection offers resources to develop grammar, vocabulary, and language fluency through engaging stories and activities. The goal of this Online Library is to promote literacy, enhance mathematical proficiency, and improve English language skills among elementary school students.

Tutorial Documentation

This video is a documentary that is part of the Himatika Universitas Airlangga YouTube channel. The video features a tutorial discussing the proof by contrapositive and the use of modulo in solving mathematical problems. The tutorial explains the concepts through problem-solving examples related to proof by contrapositive and modulo. The video provides an explanation of the modulo concept and how to use it in problem-solving, along with clear examples that illustrate its application. This tutorial video is one of the documented tutorials I have conducted. The topic of this tutorial video is tailored to the needs of students regarding the subject "Dasar-Dasar Matematika I" which is considered difficult and not fully mastered by most mathematics students in Airlangga University. With clear explanations and provided examples, it is hoped that this video can help improve students' understanding of the concepts of proof by contrapositive and the use of modulo.
